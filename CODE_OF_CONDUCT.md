# Code of Conduct

Every project under this org is built on a simple belief: good work comes from clear thinking and honest collaboration. This document defines how I expect everyone who engages here to show up.

---

## The Standard

I hold my own work to a high standard and I ask the same of everyone who contributes, comments, or participates in any capacity.

Conduct that belongs here:

- Communicating with directness and respect
- Engaging with ideas on their merit
- Giving feedback that is specific, honest, and constructive
- Accepting feedback without defensiveness
- Acknowledging when you are wrong and moving forward
- Prioritizing the quality of the work over being right

Conduct that has no place here:

- Personal attacks, insults, or dismissiveness toward any person
- Harassment of any kind, public or private
- Sharing someone's private information without their explicit consent
- Derailing discussions with irrelevant arguments or bad faith engagement
- Discriminatory language or behavior of any kind
- Sustained disruptive behavior after being asked to stop

The bar for participation is simple: engage with the work seriously and treat people with basic respect. Everything else follows from that.

---

## Enforcement

I take reports seriously. If something violates this code, I will act.

Depending on the nature of the violation, the response may include a private warning, removal of the offending content, temporary restriction from participation, or a permanent ban from all repositories under this org.

I make these calls based on what protects the quality and integrity of the work and the people doing it.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation, reach me at [connect@omrajguru.com](mailto:hello@omrajguru.com) or through [omrajguru.com/contact](https://www.omrajguru.com/contact).

Reports are handled with discretion. I will review every report and respond with a clear course of action.

---

## Scope

This code of conduct applies across all repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other space under this org. It also applies when someone is representing this org or its projects in any external context.

---

## The Bigger Picture

I want this to be a place where sharp ideas get built into real things. That requires people who take the work seriously and treat each other with honesty and respect.

If you are here to build something meaningful, you are welcome here.
